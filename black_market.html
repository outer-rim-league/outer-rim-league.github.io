  Black Market

  • Credits will be awarded for all Bounties as well as end-of-season Most Wanted List placement
  • Credits balances will be tracked on the Most Wanted List (regardless of qualification for placement)
  • At the end of the season, players can use credits to buy prizes:
    • Official FFG prize kit items, including store kits donated by Tribe as well as items donated by other players
    • Custom alt arts made by various community members
    • Set of custom painted ship bases/pegs in Matte, Gloss, or Transparent single color, made by Nick Sunderlin
    • Custom tokens made by various community members
    • And more!
  • Limited quantity items will be drafted, order determined by Notoriety
  • All of the items will be shown here by late season with credit cost; make selections within 1 week of season end
