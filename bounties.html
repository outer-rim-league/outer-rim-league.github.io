  Bounties

  • Earn 1 credit for each time you do the following [max awards]
    • Log a game [10]
    • Play a new faction for the first time [3]
    • Play a new format (Extended, Hyperspace, Quick Builds) for the first time [3]
    • Win with new faction for the first time [3]
    • Win with new format (Extended, Hyperspace, Quick Builds) for the first time [3]
    • More bounties, including week specific bounties, to be announced!
  • Use credits to buy goods off of the Black Market at the end of season
  • Values subject to change, will be finalized (and retroactively applied) by mid-season
  • Credit balance will be tracked on Most Wanted List
  • Bounties are independent of Most Wanted placement (i.e. playing 6 separate opponents not required)
