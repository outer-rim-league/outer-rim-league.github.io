  Most Wanted List

  • Win matches to earn Notieriety and climb to the top of the Outer Rim Most Wanted List
    • Play 6 opponents and come to Tribe Xwing night 3 times to qualify for end-of-season placement rewards
  • Current Notoriety method is experimental; will be finalized by midseason
    • Based on Elo/Chess Rating System
    • Both match winner and points destroyed matter, e.g. you gain 25 Notoriety for a 200-0 win against a similarly notorious pilot, 12 for a 200-100, 7 for 200-175
    • Defeating more notorious pilots gains more notoriety, defeating lesser known pilots gains less
    • Calculated on a weekly basis; order of games played/logged within a week doesn't matter
  • Additional info (credit balance, bounty completion, etc) to be added soon
  • Games with new players don't reward notoriety but do count toward number of opponents, bounties, etc
  • See games log to log games and check for any inaccuracies
